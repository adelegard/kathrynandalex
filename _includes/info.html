    Both the wedding ceremony and the reception will take place at The Minneapolis Club. The ceremony will start at 4:30pm, followed immediately by a cocktail hour outside on the patio. Whether you stay at the Minneapolis Club or another hotel in the area, you may leave your car overnight and pick it up anytime on Sunday.

    There are several hotels within walking distance to the club. We have reserved blocks of discounted rooms at the W Hotel and the Hilton. The W Hotel cannot be booked online with our reserved block. You will have to call (612) 215-3700 and reference the Scharton/Delegard block. The Hilton can be booked online with our reserved block. Once your reach the reservation page, enter the Group Code of "sdw" to receive the discount.

    During your down time, we encourage you to explore Minneapolis and enjoy all that the city has to offer in September. A few of our favorite spots are: Blackbird, the Lock and Dam, Indeed Brewery, and Sociable Ciderwerks.
